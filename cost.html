What do Green/Clean energy costs look like?

Clean energy is an energy source in which the method you’re using isn’t emitting any greenhouse gasses or pollutants into the air. Green energy however is the opposite. This is looked at to be the worst way to collect your energy. It produces a hefty amount of greenhouse gasses and pollutants into the air. This is where we bring in renewable energy. Clean energy and Green energy can be placed in the renewable energy category. Renewable energy is an energy source in which it’s emitting little to no greenhouse gasses or pollutants into the air. A great way to look at a diagram for it would be looking at a triangle in which renewable energy is at the top of the energy triangle and Clean/Green energy are on the two bottom points of the triangle. Personally, I like to look at the recycling triangle when picturing this diagram.
